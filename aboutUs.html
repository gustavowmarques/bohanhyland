About Us

Bohan Hyland and Associates are a fully bonded, licensed, and insured Property Services Provider. Established in 1969, we have proudly served our clients for over 50 years with dedication and integrity.

Our Commitment

We adhere to the highest business standards with an in-depth knowledge of the property industry, ensuring transparency and reliability.

Our Expertise

Our skilled professionals specialize in Residential & Commercial Sales, Valuations, Property Lettings, and Multi-Unit Development Management.

Our Expansion

With growing demand, we have expanded to a new office in Bettystown Town Centre to better serve our clients in Counties Meath, Louth, and Kildare.

Professional Affiliations

We are affiliated with the Society of Chartered Surveyors Ireland (SCSI), the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors (RICS), and the Property Services Regulatory Authority (PSRA).
